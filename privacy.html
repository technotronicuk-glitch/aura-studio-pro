Privacy Policy

01. Data Collection

Aura Studio collects personal information strictly for the purpose of talent representation and client bookings. This includes, but is not limited to: digital portfolios (headshots/comp cards), physical measurements, and contact credentials.

02. Talent Protection (GDPR)

We operate under strict UK and UAE data protection laws. Your portfolio is your property. Aura Studio will never sell or distribute your private contact information to third-party scouts without a signed release for a specific casting call.

03. Digital Security

All high-resolution imagery and sensitive documents are stored on encrypted, private servers. We retain model data only for the duration of active representation or until a formal request for "Right to be Forgotten" is submitted to our London or Dubai offices.

04. Cookies & Analytics

Our digital stage uses minimal tracking to enhance user experience and analyze gallery engagement. We do not use invasive advertising pixels.
